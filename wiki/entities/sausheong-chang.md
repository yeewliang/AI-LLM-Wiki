# Sau Sheong Chang

## Overview
Senior engineering leader at [[govtech-singapore]]. Writes publicly at sausheong.com on software engineering, AI adoption, and the public-sector perspective on technology change. Author of the May 2026 article "From vibe coding to agentic engineering" articulating GovTech's AI strategy.

## Why this entity matters to AI
Provides one of the more candid first-person accounts from inside a large regulated engineering organisation actually deploying AI coding tools at scale. Synthesises observations from internal teams and global industry conversations into a coherent strategic posture: AI as transitional through [[vibe-coding]] → [[agentic-engineering]] → agent orchestration, with explicit attention to bottleneck inversion (decision speed, not engineering capacity), code-as-artefact reconsideration, team-shape collapse, and [[supervisory-engineering]] as the new core competency.
