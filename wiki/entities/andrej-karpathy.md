# Andrej Karpathy

## Overview
AI researcher and educator. Co-founder of OpenAI (2015), former Director of AI / head of Autopilot Vision at Tesla (2017–2022), returned to OpenAI (2023–2024), now independent. Known for making deep technical ideas accessible through writing, teaching, and open-source work (micrograd, nanoGPT, llm.c).

## Why this entity matters to AI
Karpathy is one of the field's most influential communicators. His tutorials on neural networks and transformers have trained a generation of practitioners. He coined the term "vibe coding" in early 2026 to describe LLM-assisted code generation driven by intent rather than explicit specification.

In April 2026, he published the [[llm-wiki-pattern]] idea file (`llm-wiki.md`) as a GitHub Gist, describing the pattern for LLM-maintained personal knowledge bases. The post went viral: 16 million views, 5,000+ GitHub stars within days. He reported his own personal wiki on a single research topic had grown to ~100 articles and ~400,000 words, with minimal direct editing.

> [synthesis] Per secondary sources, Karpathy positioned the LLM Wiki as "the third phase" of human-AI collaboration — after vibe coding (AI writes code) and agentic engineering (AI executes tasks), AI manages knowledge. This framing lacks a direct citation; treat as editorial interpretation until confirmed.
